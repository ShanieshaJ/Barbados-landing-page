About Barbados

Barbados is a small island country in the south-eastern Caribbean Sea. Its neighbouring countries include Saint Lucia, to the north, Saint Vincent and Grenadines, to the west, and Trinidad and Tobago to the south. It is a fairly flat island, with Mount Hillaby rising via terraced tablelands to 336 metres. It is surrounded by coral reefs and beaches.
